[Ars Poetica #100: I Believe](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/46550/ars-poetica-100-i-believe)  

Elizabeth Alexander's personal manifesto about the nature, purpose, and raw humanity of poetry. 

> "and are we not of interest to each other?"


She is one of only six poets  in United States history to read a poem at a presidential inauguration (a list which includes Robert Frost and Maya Angelou). She read for Obama's inauguration in 2009.
