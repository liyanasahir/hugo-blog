Two quotes from Wittgenstein that I think about together:

> "The limits of my language mean the limits of my world."
> – *Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus* (1921)

If I can't say it, I can't think it; my language *is* the fence around my world. The second is about what happens when you try to climb the fence anyway. You get bruises.
